Popular Destinations

Taj Mahal, Agra

Iconic symbol of love

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Jaipur, Rajasthan

The Pink City

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is known for its royal heritage, stunning palaces, and vibrant bazaars.

Goa

Beaches and nightlife

Goa is famous for its golden beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage.

Ladakh

Himalayan adventure

Ladakh, known as the "Land of High Passes," is famous for its breathtaking landscapes and Buddhist monasteries.

Kerala

Backwaters and greenery

Kerala, often called "God's Own Country," is renowned for its serene backwaters, lush greenery, and Ayurvedic traditions.

Varanasi

Spiritual capital

Varanasi, one of the oldest cities in the world, is a major spiritual center known for its ghats along the Ganges River.

Hampi, Karnataka

Ancient ruins and temples

Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is famous for its ancient ruins, temples, and the historic Vijayanagara Empire.

Madurai, Tamil Nadu

Home to Meenakshi Temple

Madurai is an ancient city known for the grand Meenakshi Amman Temple and its rich cultural heritage.

Pondicherry

French colonial charm

Pondicherry, or Puducherry, blends French colonial architecture with Indian culture, offering a unique coastal experience.

Coorg, Karnataka

Scenic coffee plantations

Coorg, also known as Kodagu, is a hill station famous for its coffee plantations, misty hills, and waterfalls.

Paris, France

City of romance

Paris, the capital of France, is celebrated for its art, culture, and landmarks like the Eiffel Tower.

Bali, Indonesia

Tropical paradise

Bali is an Indonesian island known for its lush landscapes, beaches, and vibrant Hindu culture.
